My Art Journey

Every piece I create is a visual diary — a blend of emotion, memory, and imagination. My art is not just about what you see, but what you feel.

Each stroke, color, and shadow is chosen with purpose — sometimes bold and direct, other times subtle and introspective. Through my work, I explore transformation, identity, time, and nature. Here are some of the mediums I've explored:

Digital Art

Created using Procreate on iPad with custom brushes, airbrush glow effects, and layered textures. Every stroke is intentional, blending tech precision with raw emotion.

Pencil Drawing

Sketched with graphite pencils, shaded with smudging tools and fingertips. Light and shadow come alive through delicate lines and textured hatching.

Acrylic & Oil Painting

Thick brushwork, bold color, layered textures. From palette knife edges to smooth oil blends, this piece breathes with tactile energy and rich expression.
